Project Summary

During a 3 month coding bootcamp at Northcoders, I completed a final group project named TetriPlan. TetriPlan was a prototype desktop productivity tool designed to meet your time management needs. For the project itslef, we managed the project in a scrum agile environment where we used a kanban approach to managing our project, utlising Trello to keep track of our tasks. TetriPlan was named after Tetris, seeing our tasks as building blocks and that inspired our design and approach to UI interface. With AI capabilities utilising tensorflow.js since javascript was our language instead of Python. In terms of future plans I plan to develop the desing interface and AI capabilities of TetriPlan.
