Project Summary

Whilst working as a free-lance digital designer, I undertook a design project with the objective of crafting a website for their restaurant, Jaflong. The directive was to encapsulate elements of indian cuisine, richness of food, and home comfort. Emphasising the clarity of the title "Jaflong" and utilising the color palette of red, green, and black were integral aspects of the project. In alignment with the ownder's preference for simplicity and a contemporary aesthetic, I initiated the design process by conducting extensive research on emerging trends, contemporary logo designs, and imagery associated with indian cuisine and home comfort.

Presented below is the final design of the website, which originated from an exploration of the logo incorporating the specified color scheme.
