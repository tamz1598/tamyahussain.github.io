Project Summary

I undertook a freelance project commissioned by a client to develop branding and a logo for their emerging hijama enterprise. Hijama is an ancient practise used to drain bad blood from the body, said to eleviate pain and increase better blood circulation. The objective was to encapsulate themes of health, patience, and meditation. The title "Heal with Hijama" was coined by the clientele, to portray the service's healing and competent attributes, promoting a valuable but natural remedy to main day to day pains.
